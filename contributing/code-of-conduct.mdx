OWASP Nest follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct.html), version 2.0. All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to uphold these standards in every community space — GitHub issues and pull requests, OWASP Slack channels, and any official representation of the project.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in OWASP Nest a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

### Positive behaviour

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others.
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community.
- Accepting responsibility, apologising, and learning from mistakes.

### Unacceptable behaviour

- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwanted advances.
- Trolling, insults, derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

All OWASP Nest community leaders are responsible for enforcing these standards. They have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Enforcement follows a graduated response:

| Level | Impact | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| **Correction** | Inappropriate language or unprofessional behaviour | Private written warning with explanation |
| **Warning** | A single incident or series of actions | Warning with conditions; no contact with those involved for a specified period |
| **Temporary ban** | Serious or sustained inappropriate behaviour | Temporary ban from all community interaction |
| **Permanent ban** | Pattern of violations, harassment, or aggression | Permanent ban from all public community interaction |

## Reporting violations

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code of conduct, report it to the OWASP Nest community leaders. Contact details are listed in the **Leaders** section on [https://owasp.org/www-project-nest](https://owasp.org/www-project-nest).

All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

<Note>
All OWASP Nest project leaders are ISC2 certified security professionals, committed to maintaining the highest standards of professional conduct.
</Note>
